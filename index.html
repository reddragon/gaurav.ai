About Me
I am a Software Engineer at Facebook, where I am a part of the Search team. Specifically, I am in the Search Ranking sub-team, where I work on problems related to Machine Learning, Information Retrieval, and NLP. We help power 1.5 Billion searches a day on Facebook, and my work affects a majority of these searches.

Before Search, I worked on HBase, a peta-byte scale distributed key-value store, which powers Facebook Messages, and other important systems at Facebook.

Before joining Facebook, I was a Graduate Student at the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University. There, I worked under Prof. Radu Sion, at the Network Security and Applied Cryptography lab. Before coming to Stony Brook, I was a Software Engineer at Accenture.
